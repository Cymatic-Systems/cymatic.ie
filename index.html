What We Do

Designing, building, and operating reliable systems for performance-critical workloads.

Cymatic Systems is a Dublin-based software engineering consultancy working with teams that operate complex, performance-sensitive systems. We design, build, and run software across game development, distributed systems, networking, and cloud infrastructure.
