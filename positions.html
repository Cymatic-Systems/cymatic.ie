Software Engineer

Remote - Ireland
Posted 24th March 2022

Cymatic Systems is looking for talented software engineers with experience in building high-performance and scalable systems. We are a small team and we are looking for candidates who are excited about working in a fast-paced and collaborative environment.

You will be working with a team of engineers to build an online metaverse-inspired multiplayer gaming platform using modern software libraries and technologies. The platform will be used to host a variety of user-generated content, including games, social experiences, and user-generated media. It will be built using cutting edge technologies and we are looking for candidates who are excited about working on challenging technical problems and developing innovative solutions.

We are looking for candidates who (ideally):

  • Are comfortable with using multiple programming languages (Java, C++, Python, Rust, etc.)
  • Have experience with multi-threaded, asynchronous or reactive programming
  • Can write high-performance code
  • Interested in technical challenges
  • Excited about working in a collaborative environment

Additional experience in the following areas is a plus:

  • Network programming and protocols (TCP, UDP, HTTP, Websockets, etc.)
  • Cryptography, including public key and symmetric encryption
  • Working with databases (SQL, NoSQL)
  • Cloud infrastructure (Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, etc.)
  • Game design and development

Work Details

This role offers a competitive salary with flexible hours and the ability to work from home.

About Us

Cymatic Systems is a micro enterprise based in Dublin, Ireland that specializes in creating high-performance and scalable software systems. We take a research-based approach to solving technical problems and developing innovative solutions. Our expertise includes, but is not limited to, network protocols, game engines, and cloud infrastructure. We are always exploring new and innovative technologies and we are excited to share our knowledge and experience with others.
