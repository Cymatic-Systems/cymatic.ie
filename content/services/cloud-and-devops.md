Our cloud work starts in code. We build custom Kubernetes operators, data pipeline orchestration, and infrastructure automation — the layer between your services and the platform they run on. When we say DevOps, we mean writing software that operates your software.

## Our Expertise

*   **Platform Agnostic & Kubernetes:** Kubernetes is our deployment standard. We use managed services where they genuinely simplify operations and avoid them where they create lock-in. The goal is infrastructure your team can move between providers without rewriting.
*   **Infrastructure Automation:** We use our software engineering roots to automate operations. From custom K8s operators to automated DR failovers and complex maintenance jobs, we code the operational logic so humans don't have to.
*   **Observability & Monitoring:** We instrument services with **OpenTelemetry**, build **Prometheus**/**Grafana** dashboards that surface actionable signals, and set up alerting that pages you for the right reasons. Observability is part of the application, not an afterthought layered on top.
*   **Big Data & Streaming:** **Kafka** for real-time streaming and event sourcing, **Apache Iceberg** for analytics-ready data lakes. We design topic topologies, manage consumer groups at scale, and build the connectors and transformation layers that move data between your systems.
*   **Hybrid Networks:** We design and implement hybrid connectivity across on-premise, multi-cloud, and corporate network boundaries. Transit Gateway topologies, VPN mesh, and the routing that lets isolated environments talk to each other securely.
*   **Cost Optimization:** We treat cloud cost as an engineering problem. **Spot instance** strategies, custom auto-scaling based on real workload metrics, and resource right-sizing — applied at the infrastructure level, not as a monthly spreadsheet exercise.

We've rebuilt crashed infrastructure from scratch under pressure and migrated entire platform teams onto Kubernetes. If your infrastructure needs engineering — not just configuration — get in touch.
