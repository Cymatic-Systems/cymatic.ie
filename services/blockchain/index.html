Blockchain & Distributed Ledgers

Decentralized foundations built on security and utility.

We navigate the complexities of blockchain technology to build secure, transparent, and distributed applications. Our focus is on tangible utility and robust integration rather than speculation.

Our Expertise

  • Smart Contract Security: We prioritize safety in every line of Solidity we write. Our process involves rigorous security audits and comprehensive test coverage to mitigate risk before deployment.
  • Security Research: We actively contribute to the ecosystem's safety, with a track record of identifying critical vulnerabilities in major protocols (via platforms like Immunefi).
  • Robust Web3 Integration: We bridge the gap between traditional web/game frontends and the blockchain. Whether it's connecting a Unity game via custom RPC protocols or building a responsive dApp with Ethers.js and Thirdweb, we ensure the experience is seamless.
  • Integration Reliability: When developing blockchain projects, quick feedback is key. We test all blockchain contracts and integrations against local forks (Hardhat/Anvil) locally and in CI, ensuring your dApp works reliably before it hits a testnet.
  • Knowledge Transfer: We demystify the technology for your team, explaining the architectural trade-offs of decentralization so you can make informed product decisions.

We help you move beyond the hype, focusing on the secure implementation of distributed ledger innovations.
